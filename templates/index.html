Thendral Friends Club

A community of learners, creators and doers. Join us for events, workshops, and community projects.

The Thendral Youth Welfare Association was founded in 1996 by a dedicated group of young people and elders from the beautiful village of Christian Pet. Most villagers were farmers, united by hard work, love, and a strong desire to uplift their community.

The founding purpose of this association was clear: “Progress through Unity, Change through Love.”

What began as small efforts — village cleaning, tree planting, streetlight installation, educational support, and awareness programs — soon grew into a powerful community movement.
